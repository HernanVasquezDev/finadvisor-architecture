# Product Roadmap

## Project

EasyCredit

AI-Powered Credit Decision Platform

Version: 0.1

Status: Draft

---

# Product Philosophy

EasyCredit is built on the belief that technology should not replace financial decision-making.

Instead, it should empower users to understand their options, evaluate risks and make informed choices.

Artificial Intelligence is treated as a decision-support system, never as an autonomous decision-maker.

The user always remains in control.

---

# Roadmap Philosophy

EasyCredit is not intended to become another financial calculator.

Its long-term objective is to evolve into an AI-powered financial copilot capable of helping users make responsible financial decisions throughout their financial journey.

The roadmap is organized around product evolution rather than development iterations.

Each phase represents an increase in business value and user capabilities.

# Phase 2 — Intelligent Recommendations

## Objective

Transform recommendations into personalized financial guidance.

### New Features

- Credit eligibility estimation
- Financial health score
- Debt-to-income analysis
- Personalized borrowing limits
- Scenario comparison
- Alternative recommendations
- Goal-based recommendations

Example

Instead of saying:

"Loan A has a lower interest rate."

The platform should explain:

"Although Loan A offers a lower interest rate, Loan B results in a lower total repayment because of shorter financing and lower insurance costs."

### Success Criteria

Recommendations become contextual rather than descriptive.

# Future Vision

Future versions may include:

- Retirement planning
- Investment guidance
- Tax estimation
- Insurance recommendations
- Family financial planning
- Small business financial analysis
- AI-powered financial education

These capabilities are intentionally excluded from the MVP.

---

# Guiding Principles

Every new feature must satisfy at least one of the following objectives:

- Improve financial understanding.
- Reduce financial risk.
- Increase decision confidence.
- Promote responsible borrowing.
- Improve long-term financial well-being.

Features that do not contribute to these objectives should not be included.

---

# Definition of Success

EasyCredit succeeds when users no longer ask:

"What is my monthly payment?"

Instead, they ask:

"What is the best financial decision for my future?"

The platform's mission is not to calculate loans.

Its mission is to improve financial decision-making.
