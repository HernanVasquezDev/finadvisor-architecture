# Executive Summary

EasyCredit is an AI-powered financial decision support platform designed to help individuals understand, compare and evaluate financial products before making credit-related decisions.

Rather than acting as a simple loan calculator, EasyCredit functions as an intelligent financial assistant capable of combining traditional financial simulations with Artificial Intelligence to generate personalized recommendations, identify financial risks and explain complex financial concepts in accessible language.

The platform seeks to reduce financial uncertainty by providing users with clear, transparent and data-driven insights before acquiring debt.

---

# Problem Statement

Millions of people request loans, credit cards and financing without fully understanding:

- The real cost of borrowing.
- Hidden financial risks.
- Long-term payment implications.
- Alternative financial products.
- Their own repayment capacity.

Most existing simulators only calculate monthly payments.

They do not help users answer the most important question:

> "Should I actually take this credit?"

EasyCredit aims to answer that question.

Secondary Audience

# Value Proposition

EasyCredit transforms complex financial information into personalized, understandable and actionable recommendations.

Instead of simply presenting numbers, the platform explains what those numbers mean and how they impact the user's financial future.

# Product Goals

The platform should enable users to:

- Compare financial products.
- Simulate multiple loan scenarios.
- Understand repayment capacity.
- Identify financial risks.
- Receive AI-generated recommendations.
- Save historical analyses.
- Improve financial literacy.

---

# Out of Scope (MVP)

The first version will NOT include:

- Online loan applications.
- Investment management.
- Cryptocurrency analysis.
- Stock market recommendations.
- Insurance comparison.
- Banking integrations.
- Open Banking connectivity.
- Real-time credit bureau integration.

These capabilities may be evaluated in future releases.

# Success Metrics

Examples of product KPIs include:
